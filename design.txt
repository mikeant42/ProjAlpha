turn based, 2d, top down
- tick based

open map / world
- collectable resources with a system of progression
- in-game player trading
- medieval setting with fantasy elements

combat progression
- players can unlock new spells by finding scrolls, or finding npc's that will teach you
- each scroll acts as a "card" with a certain requirement to cast.


player progression
- players can unlock new armour by killing monsters, doing quests, trading, or crafting

questing
- various mini "quests" given along the way by npcs you will find in the world, or interferring in certain events
